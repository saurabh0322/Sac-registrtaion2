Euphoria 2025 

26th | 27th   March 2025

About Euphoria

Euphoria is the annual techno-cultural fest of SISTec Ratibad. This dynamic event features a spectrum of competitions, from high-energy activities like Robo Race, Robo War, and Drone Racing to creative expressions in Dancing, Singing, Tech Rangoli, and Face Painting. Academic prowess takes the spotlight in Branch Masters, while the day culminates in a mesmerizing Musical Evening. Join us for a day of innovation, talent, and cultural celebration!
